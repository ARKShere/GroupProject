About C.A.F.

At C.A.F. (Coffee.Alcohol.Food.) we are foodies at heart with a deep passion for flavour and convenience.
If you're looking for the hottest indie places to eat and drink in London, you've come to the right place.
We bring to you our recommended sites across the London Jubilee line - Finchley Road, London Bridge, West Hampstead and Stratford -
and we're just getting started, so watch this space!
For suggestions on where to score out next, please feel free to contact us and let us know.
Happy eating and drinking!
